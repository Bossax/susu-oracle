# Design Brief: Foresight Presentation Template – "Futures of Thai Youth 2590" (Version 2.0)

## **1. Core Concept: "The Unintended Architect"**

**Narrative Arc:** We have experienced a **Rupture** (Discontinuous Change). Old maps (Trends) no longer work. We are currently building a future of "Forced Independence" by accident. We must choose to build a future of "Supported Autonomy."

**Visual Metaphor:**

- **Act 1 (The Wake-Up Call):** High Contrast, Noir, "The Flashlight in the Dark."
    
- **Act 2 (The Blueprints):** Constructive, Warm, Heroic, "The Architect at Work."
    
- **Act 3 (The Choice):** Clean, Geometric, Structural, "The Matrix/Navigation Map."
    

**Global Fonts:**

- **Thai Headline:** _Pridi_ (Bold, Grounded).
    
- **English Headline:** _Cormorant Garamond_ (Elegant, Serious).
    
- **Body Text:** _Prompt_ (Modern, Clean).

## **2. Act 1: The Wake-Up Call (Slides 1–6)**

**Mood:** Noir, Urgent, Revealing.

**Color Palette:** `#0F0F0F` (Black), `#E0E0E0` (Grey Text), `#FF4500` (Orange/Red Accents for "Rupture").

### **Slide 1: Title Slide (The Rupture)**

- **Headline:** "อนาคตเด็กและเยาวชนในสังคมไทย พ.ศ. 2590" (Futures of Thai Youth 2590)
    
- **Subtext:** "โลกที่ไม่ต่อเนื่อง... และความรับผิดชอบของวันนี้" (A Discontinuous World... and Today's Responsibility).
    
- **Visual Concept:** A massive bridge that has snapped in half, or a road disappearing into a digital void.
    
- **Canva Keywords:** `"Broken bridge fog"`, `"Road ending in abyss"`, `"Abstract rift dark"`, `"Glitch effect overlay"`.
    

### **Slide 2: The Broken Continuity**

- **Headline:** "โลกที่ความไม่ต่อเนื่องคือความปกติใหม่" (A World Where Discontinuity is the New Normal).
    
- **Visual Concept:** A split screen or shattered glass effect.
    
    - _Left Shard:_ Faded image of an old school/factory (The Past).
        
    - _Right Shard:_ Chaotic digital storm or tangled wires (The Present).
        
- **Canva Keywords:** `"Shattered glass black background"`, `"Old vs new world contrast"`, `"Tangled wires abstract"`.
    

### **Slide 3: Foresight $\neq$ Prediction (The Pivot)**

- **Headline:** "การคาดการณ์ไม่ใช่การทำนาย... แต่คือความรับผิดชอบ" (Foresight is not Prediction... It is Responsibility).
    
- **Visual Concept:** A single, sharp beam of light (flashlight) cutting through thick fog. The light reveals a child standing in the dark.
    
- **Canva Keywords:** `"Flashlight beam in dark"`, `"Lighthouse beam fog"`, `"Silhouette child in spotlight"`.
    
- **Element:** `"Light ray overlay"`.
    

### **Slide 4: The 4 Lenses of Change**

- **Headline:** "เลนส์ใหม่ในการมองอนาคต" (New Lenses for the Future).
    
- **Visual Concept:** Four distinct glass panes or prisms floating in a dark void. Each contains a subtle icon.
    
    1. **Risk (ความเสี่ยง):** Storm icon (Structural Risk).
        
    2. **Trust (ความไว้วางใจ):** Handshake or Network node (Shift to Person-based).
        
    3. **Rights (สิทธิ):** Scale or Shield (Needs reinterpretation).
        
    4. **Inequality (ความเหลื่อมล้ำ):** Ladder with missing rungs (Access to opportunity).
        
- **Canva Keywords:** `"Floating glass panes"`, `"Prism abstract"`, `"Abstract geometric 3d dark"`.
    

### **Slide 5: The Great Divergence (Signals)**

- **Headline:** "สัญญาณการแยกขั้ว: ระบบ vs คน" (The Great Divergence: System vs. Person).
    
- **Visual Concept:** The **"Split Reality"**.
    
    - _Left Side (Darker/Red tint):_ "Person-Dependent" (Fragile). Image: A child climbing a rock face alone.
        
    - _Right Side (Lighter/Blue tint):_ "System-Dependent" (Robust). Image: A child climbing a wall with a harness/rope.
        
- **Canva Keywords:** `"Climbing without rope"`, `"Safety net abstract"`, `"Split screen contrast"`.
    

### **Slide 6: The Transition (Handover)**

- **Headline:** "จากสัญญาณ... สู่ฉากทัศน์" (From Signals... To Scenarios).
    
- **Visual Concept:** A hand passing a glowing object (a blueprint or light) to another hand. The background shifts from Black to the First Group's Color (Red/Brown).
    
- **Canva Keywords:** `"Passing baton light"`, `"Glowing blueprint roll"`, `"Architects looking at horizon"`.

## **3. Act 2: The Blueprints (Group Intros)**

**Mood:** Heroic, Constructive, "The Poster".
**Layout:** Large Typography + Monochromatic Hero Image.

Here is the **Act 2: The Blueprints** design guide. This section transforms the presentation from a "Noir Thriller" (Act 1) into an **"Action / Strategy Movie."**

Each group uses the **same 5-slide structural template** to ensure consistency, but applies their specific **"Thematic Skin"** (Color + Iconography) to distinguish their domain.

### **Universal Design Logic for Act 2**

- **The Metaphor:** **"The Repair Crew."** If Act 1 was the "Rupture" (The Earthquake), Act 2 is the reconstruction.
    
- **Visual Style:** **Clean, Technical, Heroic.** Move away from "grainy/foggy" textures. Use clear lines, blueprints, and bright focal points.
    
- **Consistency:** Every group must follow this exact sequence.
    

### **Slide 1: The System Map (Leverage Points)**

**Concept:** _The Constellation._ Do not show a messy "spaghetti" map. Show only the 4 bright stars (Leverage Points) that matter in the dark sky.

- **Layout:**
    
    - **Background:** Deep, rich version of the Group Color (e.g., Dark Midnight Green for Development).
        
    - **Visual:** 4 glowing "Nodes" or icons arranged in a diamond or organic shape, connected by thin, pulsating lines.
        
    - **Text:** Numbered 01-04. Minimal text.
        
- **Canva Keywords:**
    
    - `"Constellation nodes connection"`
        
    - `"Network topology minimalist"`
        
    - `"Glowing geometric points"`
        

### **Slide 2: Critical Uncertainties (The Fog of War)**

**Concept:** _The Compass._ What factors will spin the future?

- **Layout:**
    
    - **Visual:** A central abstract object representing "Balance" or "Flux" (e.g., a gyroscope, a floating compass, a prism).
        
    - **Composition:** The 4 uncertainties float around this center, creating a sense of tension.
        
    - **Text:** Phrased as "X vs Y" (e.g., "Tech vs Human").
        
- **Canva Keywords:**
    
    - `"Gyroscope abstract 3d"`
        
    - `"Balance scale minimalist"`
        
    - `"Floating geometric shapes tension"`
        

### **Slide 3: Scenarios (The Divergence)**

**Concept:** _The Sliding Doors._ The moment the path splits.

- **Layout:**
    
    - **Visual:** A **Diagonal Split Screen**.
        
        - **Top Left (The Trap):** Darker, monochrome, high contrast (Represents the "Forced Independence" future).
            
        - **Bottom Right (The Goal):** Brighter, saturated, soft light (Represents the "Supported Autonomy" future).
            
    - **Element:** A visible "rift" or line separating them.
        
- **Canva Keywords:**
    
    - `"Diagonal split screen overlay"`
        
    - `"Light vs dark abstract"`
        
    - `"Two worlds contrast"`
        

### **Slide 4: Vision & Preferred Future (The Quest)**

**Concept:** _The Postcard from 2590._ A glimpse of the "Resilient World."

- **Layout:**
    
    - **Visual:** Full-bleed, high-quality "Hero Shot." A child/youth actively engaged in that right (e.g., voting, planting, safe at home).
        
    - **Lighting:** The brightest slide in the deck. Golden hour or clean morning light.
        
    - **Text:** The Vision Statement in large _Cormorant Garamond_ font, floating in the negative space (sky/wall).
        
- **Canva Keywords:**
    
    - `"Cinematic portrait child hopeful"`
        
    - `"Futuristic sustainable city bright"`
        
    - `"Sunrise horizon open"`
        

### **Slide 5: Strategic Pathways (The Blueprint)**

**Concept:** _The Bridge._ How we get from "Rupture" to "Resilience."

- **Layout:**
    
    - **Visual:** A clear directional graphic moving **Up and to the Right**. A staircase, a bridge, or a roadmap.
        
    - **Graphic:** Use "Milestone Markers" (flags or pins) for the steps.
        
    - **Style:** Technical but clean. Blueprint aesthetic.
        
- **Canva Keywords:**
    
    - `"Staircase rising abstract"`
        
    - `"Roadmap milestones isometric"`
        
    - `"Bridge construction schematic"`

### **Visual Transition Note**

- **Between Groups:** Use a 2-second **"Wipe" transition** or **"Color Fade"** in your presentation software to signal the changing of the guard (e.g., Red fades to Black $\rightarrow$ Black fades to Green). This cleans the palate for the audience.
    


## **4. Act 3: The Choice (Closing Slides)**

**Mood:** Analytical, Geometric, Decisive.

**Color Palette:** White, Light Grey, Navy Blue (Trust/Structure).

### **Slide 1: The Strategic Matrix**

- **Headline:** "ฉากทัศน์อนาคต: เรากำลังสร้างโลกใบไหน?" (Future Scenarios: Which World Are We Building?).
    
- **Visual Concept:** A clean, large 2x2 Grid.
    
    - _X-Axis:_ Access via **Person (คน)** $\leftrightarrow$ Access via **System (ระบบ)**.
        
    - _Y-Axis:_ Growth via **Bonding (ความผูกพัน)** $\leftrightarrow$ Growth via **Independence (อิสระ)**.
        
- **Canva Keywords:** `"Grid matrix clean"`, `"Coordinate system minimal"`.
    

### **Slide 2: The Trap (Forced Independence)**

- **Headline:** "กับดัก: ถูกบังคับให้อิสระ" (The Trap: Forced Independence).
    
- **Visual Concept:** Highlight the **Top-Left Quadrant** (Independence + Person/No System).
    
- **Image:** A lone tightrope walker with no net.
    
- **Text Highlight:** "ระบบไม่รองรับ... ความเสี่ยงเป็นเรื่องส่วนบุคคล" (System doesn't support... Risk is personal).
    
- **Canva Keywords:** `"Tightrope walker dangerous"`, `"Alone in big city"`.
    

### **Slide 3: The Goal (Supported Autonomy)**

- **Headline:** "เป้าหมาย: อิสระบนฐานระบบ" (The Goal: Supported Autonomy).
    
- **Visual Concept:** Highlight the **Top-Right Quadrant** (Independence + System).
    
- **Image:** A child running freely in a safe, open field or a futuristic safe city.
    
- **Text Highlight:** "ระบบเปิด... ความผิดพลาดไม่ตัดโอกาส" (Open System... Failure is not fatal).
    
- **Canva Keywords:** `"Child running freedom"`, `"Futuristic green city safe"`.
    

### **Slide 4: The Final Question**

- **Headline:** "เรากำลังคาดหวังความเป็นอิสระ โดยให้หลักประกันเพียงพอหรือไม่?" (Are We Expecting Independence Without Providing Security?).
    
- **Visual Concept:** A simple, powerful image of a child looking directly at the camera (the audience).
    
- **Text:** Large, centered question.
    
- **Canva Keywords:** `"Portrait of thai child serious"`, `"Direct eye contact child"`.
