# National Climate Adaptation Information Framework (NCAIF) - Option 1: Thematic-Based

This artifact outlines the key content and structure for a thematic-based NCAIF.

## 1. Home

The homepage will serve as the main entry point to the NCAIF, providing a high-level overview of national climate risks and easy access to the latest information.

*   **National Climate Risk Overview:** A dynamic map and dashboard summarizing the key climate risks facing Thailand.
*   **Latest Updates and News:** A feed of recent news, publications, and events related to climate change adaptation in Thailand.
*   **Featured Content:** Highlights of key resources, such as new reports, case studies, or decision-support tools.

## 2. Climate Risks & Impacts

This section will provide detailed information on climate change risks and impacts, organized by hazard and sector.

*   **By Hazard:** In-depth information on specific climate hazards, including historical data, future projections, and vulnerability maps.
    *   Floods
    *   Droughts
    *   Sea Level Rise
    *   Extreme Heat
*   **By Sector:** Analysis of climate change impacts on key sectors of the Thai economy and society.
    *   Agriculture
    *   Water Resources
    *   Public Health
    *   Tourism
    *   Infrastructure
*   **Provincial Risk Profiles:** Detailed risk profiles for each province, providing localized information for decision-makers.

## 3. Adaptation Planning

This section will provide resources and guidance to support the development and implementation of adaptation plans.

*   **National Adaptation Plan (NAP):** The full text of Thailand's NAP, along with related policy documents and progress reports.
*   **Adaptation Options Library:** A searchable database of adaptation options, categorized by type.
    *   Nature-Based Solutions
    *   Engineering Solutions
    *   Policy & Governance
*   **Case Studies & Best Practices:** A collection of case studies from Thailand and around the world, showcasing successful adaptation initiatives.
*   **Decision Support Tools:** Interactive tools to help users assess climate risks and identify appropriate adaptation options.

## 4. Data & Resources

This section will provide access to a wide range of data, publications, and other resources.

*   **Data Catalog:** A searchable catalog of climate-related datasets from various sources.
*   **Map Viewer:** An interactive map viewer for visualizing geospatial data.
*   **Publications & Reports:** A library of research papers, reports, and other publications.
*   **Glossary:** A glossary of key terms related to climate change and adaptation.
