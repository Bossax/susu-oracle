## Inline Hydrophone Frame

## Simple, protected recordings start here.  
  
The Inline Hydrophone Frame secures your hydrophones, isolates vibration, and keeps them performing at their best in inline deployments.  
  
With shackles on either end to easily connect to the Acoustic Buoy, and rubber fittings to hold the hydrophone in place, this frame is designed for optimal hydrophone deployments.

Features & Benefits

- **Robust, Corrosion-Resistant Materials:** Constructed from marine-grade stainless steel to withstand harsh saltwater environments.
- **Streamlined, Hydrodynamic Design:** Minimizes flow noise and turbulence to maintain acoustic data integrity.
- **Secure Hydrophone Mounting:** Precisely engineered to hold icListen hydrophones firmly, preventing movement or vibration.
- **Compact and Lightweight:** Designed for easy handling, transport, and deployment.
- **Protects Hydrophones During Deployment:** Guards against physical impacts, abrasion, and entanglement.
